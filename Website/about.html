About ABL

AudioBook.Love grew out of the constant frustration and time spent trying to locate an audiobook to listen to. The creator is a former librarian, current tech person, and avid reader and listener of audiobooks. However, finding where an audiobook is available takes time, energy, and potentially money.


To find a way to make this process much easier, AudioBook.Love was born. We want to make that search easier, faster, and as painless as possible so that you can continue what you love; reading/listening!
